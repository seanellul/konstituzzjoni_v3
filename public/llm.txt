# Kostituzzjoni.mt - LLM Context Documentation

## Project Overview
This is an interactive web application for exploring and understanding the Constitution of Malta, hosted on both constitution.mt and kostituzzjoni.mt domains. Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, it provides a modern, user-friendly interface to access Malta's founding legal document in Malta's bilingual environment.

## Key Features
- Interactive Navigation: Browse through constitutional chapters and articles with intuitive interface
- Advanced Search: Full-text search across all constitutional content
- Legal Cross-References: Understand connections between different articles and legal concepts
- Visual Data Insights: Interactive charts and graphs for constitutional elements
- Responsive Design: Optimized for all devices and accessibility standards
- SEO Optimized: Comprehensive SEO implementation for legal and constitutional terms

## Constitutional Content Structure

The Constitution of Malta is organized into 11 main chapters:

1. Chapter I: The Republic of Malta (Articles 1-6)
2. Chapter II: Declaration of Principles (Articles 7-21)
3. Chapter III: Citizenship (Articles 22-31)
4. Chapter IV: Fundamental Rights and Freedoms (Articles 32-47)
5. Chapter V: The President (Articles 48-50)
6. Chapter VI: Parliament (Articles 51-77)
7. Chapter VII: The Executive (Articles 78-94)
8. Chapter VIII: The Judiciary (Articles 95-101A)
9. Chapter IX: Finance (Articles 102-108)
10. Chapter X: The Public Service (Articles 109-115)
11. Chapter XA: Local Councils (Article 115A)
12. Chapter XI: Miscellaneous (Articles 116-124)
